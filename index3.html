Jinx's Arsenal

Welcome to the mind of yours truly, Jinx! I know you’re here ‘cause you’re curious about my lovely little arsenal of chaos and how I use it to turn frowns upside-down (and buildings inside-out). Lucky for you, I’m feeling extra generous today. Let’s talk about my beauties, shall we?

Meet the Familie

Fishbones

-Big. Loud. BOOM! Fishbones is my favorite, and I’m not even sorry about it. She’s a shark-shaped masterpiece that can level a room—or a block.

-Perfect for when I wanna make a BIG entrance or send a little love letter to Piltover’s snobs.

-She’s not just for destruction; Fishbones makes a statement. Who else has a rocket launcher that doubles as art?

-Versatile? Oh, you bet! Whether it’s a direct hit or blowing up something nearby, Fishbones always leaves her mark.

-Carrying her around makes me feel unstoppable. I mean, who’s gonna argue with a rocket launcher shaped like a shark?

Pow Pow

-When you need to really get the point across (and by point, I mean a zillion bullets), Pow-Pow is the gal for the job.

-She’s great for those up-close, “HI THERE, BYE NOW!” moments.

-Once she starts spinning, it’s like a symphony of chaos—music to my ears, and bad news for everyone else.

-Perfect for shredding through anything that gets in my way, whether it’s enemies or their precious little defenses.

Pow-Pow isn’t just a minigun; she’s a friend. Reliable, fierce, and always ready to unleash mayhem on command.

Zapper

-Pew pew! Zapper doesn’t just zap—it STUNS.

-I love watching people’s faces when they realize they can’t move. It’s priceless!

She’s my go-to for those quick, “Oops, you’re not going anywhere” moments—paralyzing perfection.

Zapper’s shock isn’t just powerful; it’s precise. One squeeze, and BAM—good luck running away!

Compact and stylish, Zapper’s always ready for a little electrifying mischief whenever I need her.

Flame Chomper

-Say hello to my little friends! They chomp, they explode, and they trap anyone dumb enough to step on ‘em.

-Perfect for when I want to make someone stay right where I can see ‘em (and by “see,” I mean “blow up”).

-They’re not just grenades—they’ve got personality! Each one’s like a tiny, explosive pet with a fiery bite.

-Great for setting traps or creating a fiery little surprise party for my enemies.

-Watching people panic when they’re stuck with a chomper? Oh, that’s just the cherry on top!
